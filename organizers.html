Organizers

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is an international, non-profit, apolitical and non-governmental student volunteer organization. Since its founding in 1989, BEST has brought together more than 5 000 students of STEM faculties across Europe to gain experience and knowledge through various projects and seminars.

LBG Belgrade was founded in November 2003 by enthusiastic students with the same goal which is to support young people on their career path. This year in July, we are organizing a BEST Belgrade Course in summer on Data science for 22 students all around Europe. Participants will get the chance to improve their skills and knowledge in this field of science, enjoy our special social activities and spend 10 unforgettable days in Belgrade!
