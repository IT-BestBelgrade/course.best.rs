Meet the team behind this year’s BEST Course in Belgrade

For the 18th time in a row, BEST Belgrade is organizing an international academic seminar, this year on the topic of data science and its application. The seminar is called "The Big DATA Theory - Multiverse of Science" and will be held from July 15 to 22, when 22 students from 11 European countries will visit Belgrade and have the opportunity to master the basics of data science, but also get to know Serbia and Serbian culture.


We present to you the organizational team that will make sure that this year’s Course is successfully realized:


Main Organizer - Marija Milić, a student at the Faculty of Organizational Sciences
Academic Responsible - Tamara Stamatović, a student at the Faculty of Mathematics
Company Relations Coordinator - Isidora Ćojbašić, a student at the Faculty of Technology and Metallurgy
Public Relations and Design Coordinator - Aleksa Dogandžić, a student at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering
